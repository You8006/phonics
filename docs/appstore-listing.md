## 説明文 (Description)

### 英語 (English)

```
Phonics Sense — Master all 42 English sounds through interactive games and exercises.

Perfect for beginners, language learners, and children! Learn phonics step-by-step with a scientifically-designed spaced repetition system.

LEARN
• 7 progressive steps — from basic vowels to tricky digraphs
• Hear each sound with 3 voice options: Female, Male, and Young Speaker
• See example words for every sound with IPA notation

PLAY & PRACTICE
• Sound Quiz — Listen and pick the correct letter
• IPA Quiz — Match sounds to their IPA symbols
• Bingo — Complete the board by identifying sounds
• Blending — Build words by arranging letters
• Word Chaining — Change one sound to make new words
• Minimal Pairs — Train your ear to catch subtle differences
• Fill-in Quiz — Use phonics knowledge to complete words
• Sound Explorer — See a letter, find its sound

WORD LIBRARY
• Browse and listen to 100+ essential English words
• Organized by phonics categories
• Search and filter by spelling patterns

SMART REVIEW
• Spaced repetition system automatically schedules reviews
• Track your learning streak and progress statistics
• Sounds you miss are added to your personal review queue

FEATURES
• Works offline — all sounds included in the app
• 6 languages — Japanese, English, Spanish, Portuguese, Chinese, Hindi
• No account required — no ads — no in-app purchases
• 100% free and privacy-friendly — no data collection
```

## App Store スクリーンショット

### 必要なサイズ

| デバイス                 | サイズ (px) | 必須           |
| ------------------------ | ----------- | -------------- |
| iPhone 6.7" (15 Pro Max) | 1290 × 2796 | ✅             |
| iPhone 6.5" (11 Pro Max) | 1242 × 2688 | 推奨           |
| iPad Pro 12.9"           | 2048 × 2732 | iPad対応の場合 |

### 推奨するスクリーンショット構成 (5〜10枚)

1. **ホーム画面** — 学習ステップ一覧 + 進捗統計
2. **レッスン画面** — 音の学習カード（IPA + 例単語）
3. **サウンドクイズ** — ゲーム中の画面
4. **ビンゴ** — ビンゴボードのプレイ中
5. **ワードライブラリ** — 単語リスト画面
6. **ブレンディング** — 文字を並べている場面
7. **設定画面** — 音声選択・言語選択
8. **リザルト画面** — スコア表示

### スクリーンショットの撮り方（Windows環境）

Web版でブラウザの DevTools を使用:

1. `flutter run -d chrome --web-renderer canvaskit` で起動
2. Chrome DevTools → Toggle device toolbar (Ctrl+Shift+M)
3. iPhone 14 Pro Max (430 × 932) を選択
4. Ctrl+Shift+P → "Capture screenshot" で保存
5. 画像を 1290 × 2796 にリサイズ
