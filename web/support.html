Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my learning progress?

Go to Settings → "Reset Learning Data". This will delete all scores, streaks, and history. This action cannot be undone.

Q: Can I change the voice?

Yes! Go to Settings → Voice Settings. You can choose from Female (Jenny), Male (Andrew), or Young Speaker (Ana).

Q: What languages does the app support?

The app interface is available in Japanese, English, Spanish, Portuguese, Chinese (Simplified), and Hindi. The phonics content teaches English sounds regardless of the interface language.

Q: Does the app work offline?

Yes, all phonics sounds and exercises work offline. An internet connection may only be needed for loading fonts on the first launch.

Q: Is the app free?

Yes, Phonics Sense is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases.

Q: Is my data safe?

All your learning data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
